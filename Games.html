Minecraft

Minecraft is a voxel-based sandbox video game, the brainchild of the brilliant Swedish programmer Markus Persson, also known as “Notch.” Developed and published by Mojang Studios, which is now a subsidiary of Xbox Game Studios, Minecraft launched its early versions in 2009, before fully releasing in 2011.
